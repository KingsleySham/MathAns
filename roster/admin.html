Step 1 · Dates

Pick the date range, weekdays, and any specific blockout dates.

Step 2 · Times

Choose the basic time band (e.g. 3:30pm – 5:30pm) which auto-splits into 30-min slots. Add any custom slots with titles below.

Give a slot a title (e.g. "Warm-up") or add a custom-period slot. These are added on top of the basic time band.

Step 3 · Day overrides

Sometimes a date doesn't fit all time slots (e.g. only morning that day). Skip times for specific dates here. Leave empty to apply all times to all dates.

Step 4 · Settings

Format, reasons, and check-in policy.

When online is selected, you can manage Zoom meeting links from the Zoom Meetings tab. Students see a "Join Zoom" button on their dashboard.
