What we collect

The Orchestral Frame is a local-first roster tool. We collect strictly the following fields from you, and only when you voluntarily enter them:

  • personName — the name you type during onboarding or are added under by an admin.
  • theater_comedyRole / Part — optional. Purely informational.
  • event_availableAvailability & blockouts — the dates and half-hour slots you tag.
  • loginCheck-in timestamps — when you confirm attendance.
  • event_busyLeave requests — the rehearsal and reason you submit.

We do not collect: email, phone, address, IP, device fingerprints, location, analytics identifiers, photos, or biometric data.

2

How it's used

Your information is used exclusively to:

schedule

Schedule rehearsals

Recommend slots that work for the most people.

fingerprint

Generate your Script ID

So you can return without creating an account.

fact_check

Track attendance

Check-ins & leave reasons for the production team only.

3

Where it lives

All data is stored in this browser's localStorage. Nothing is transmitted to an external server. Clearing your browser data erases your entry. We do not sell, share, or disclose data to third parties, and we never use it for marketing, tracking, or advertising.
