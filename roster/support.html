Your Script ID is your key

After onboarding we assign you a Script ID like /student01. Use it on the landing page to sign back in from any device. No password, no email — just your ID.

Mark your availability

On the Availability page you'll see a week view. Tap a slot once to mark yourself available, tap again to mark it blocked, and tap a third time to clear.

Let the system recommend

Slots highlighted in purple are the ones most of the cast can attend. Tap a recommendation to instantly mark yourself available. Admins use the same ranking on the Dashboard to lock in a rehearsal.

Check in at rehearsal

On your Rehearsal Hub you'll see a live countdown to the next session. From 30 minutes before the call time, the Check in button activates — one tap to log your arrival.

Requesting leave

Can't make it? Tap Request leave, give a short reason, and the production team will see it on the Dashboard. Reasons are only visible to admins.

Frequently asked

Will my name be visible to other cast members? expand_more

No — only the production admin sees the full cast ledger. On shared views, your availability is aggregated anonymously.

What if I clear my browser data? expand_more

Your Script ID and availability are stored locally. Clearing site data will remove them — ask the admin to re-add you to the ledger if needed.

Can I change my Script ID? expand_more

Script IDs are permanent for the production run to keep scheduling consistent. An admin can re-issue one if yours is lost.

Need human help? Ask your production admin — they have access to the full Dashboard and can reset availability, re-issue IDs, or process leave requests.
